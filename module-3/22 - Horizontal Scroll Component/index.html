Horizontal Scroll Widget

In most cases, our job as responsive web developers is to make sure our content doesn't overflow its container (let alone the viewport).

But what if we're making an online shop and want to build a widget that suggests similar products? We might decide that we want to let our users scroll through a catalogue, but don't want it to take up as much room as everything in a search results or product category page. This is where horizontal scroll widgets come in.
